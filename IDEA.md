# Aithera

Aithera is the idea that your computer should have a mind.

Not a chatbot you open in a tab. Not a plugin inside another app.
A persistent intelligence that lives on your machine, knows your context,
manages your time, reads your communications, and acts on your behalf —
the way a real assistant would, but available always, accountable only to you.

The vision is a single point of contact for everything: you talk to Aithera,
and Aithera handles the rest. It reads your email and decides what matters.
It checks your calendar before committing to anything. It executes tasks,
runs agents, remembers what you told it last week, and reaches you wherever
you are — desktop, Telegram, eventually anywhere.

The name and the ambition come from the same place: an entity that grows
with you, learns your patterns, anticipates your needs, and gets smarter
the more you use it. Not a tool you configure once. A presence that evolves.

Aithera is being built as a personal project, for one user, with the
discipline of a product — because the best way to build something that
truly serves you is to build it for yourself first.
